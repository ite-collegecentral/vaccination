Shortening the interval between 1st and 2nd Vaccination doses for ITE students

Dear Student,

We thank you for participating in the vaccination exercise.

MOH has announced on 29 June 2021 that the interval between the first and second vaccine doses can be shortened to 4 weeks, and all individuals with scheduled second dose appointments 6 to 8 weeks after their first dose appointment can now bring forward these appointments.

For ITE students, the interval between the two doses will be brought forward to about 4 weeks. Your Class Advisors will be communicating with you on the revised appointment date and timeslots for your second dose.

Let us continue to stand together in fighting this pandemic, and keep our institutions, our community and our loved ones safe.

Dr Ting Kok Guan

Director / College Services / ITE College Central
